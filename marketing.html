Marketing and transparency statement

I am an early-stage independent developer building Joker’s Crystal Rush. This page explains what the app is, how I approach value, and how future monetization may work.

What the app aims to deliver

  • A clear, straightforward gameplay experience with responsive controls.
  • Privacy-first design: no ads, no tracking, no data collection.
  • Consistent updates based on player feedback and bug reports.
indie developer privacy-first no ads no tracking

Monetization (current and future)

  • Currently: no subscriptions and no paid features.
  • Future: optional paid content may be introduced to support development and generate income.
  • If introduced, purchases will be clearly described and always optional.
no subscriptions (now) optional purchases (future) transparent pricing

How I try to create value

  • Reduce friction: fast load, quick sessions, clear progression.
  • Respect time: meaningful challenges without manipulation or hidden tracking.
  • Support: direct contact with the developer via email.
