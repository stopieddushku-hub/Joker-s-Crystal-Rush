Privacy Policy

Joker’s Crystal Rush is built with privacy as a default. The app does not collect personal data and does not share data with third parties.

Data collection

  • No personal data is collected.
  • No analytics SDKs are used.
  • No advertising SDKs are used.
  • No identifiers for tracking are created or stored.

Data storage

  • No data is uploaded to servers.
  • Any game progress (if present) is stored locally on the device only.
  • No accounts are required.

Data sharing

  • No data is sold, shared, or transferred to third parties.
  • No cross-app tracking.
