Privacy promise

Support is provided without changing the privacy-first approach: no data collection, no tracking, and no sharing.

App is lagging or stuttering

Close other apps, reboot the device, and try again. If possible, include your device model and Android version in your message.

Audio or vibration not working

Check system sound and vibration settings as well as any in-app toggles. Some devices limit haptics in power saving mode.

Progress reset

The app stores data locally on the device. Clearing app storage or reinstalling the app may reset saved progress.

Report a bug

Send an email with a short description, exact steps, and screenshots if possible. Reports are reviewed directly by the developer.
