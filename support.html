Quick troubleshooting

App is lagging or stuttering

Close other apps, reboot the device, and try again. If you can, share your device model and Android version.

Audio or vibration not working

Check system sound/vibration settings and in-app toggles. Some devices restrict haptics in power saving mode.

Progress reset

The app stores data locally on the device. Clearing app storage or reinstalling may reset progress.

Report a bug

Send an email with a clear description, exact steps, and screenshots if possible. I review every report personally.
